Turtles

Sri lanka turtle population

A wide variety of turtle species can be found in Sri Lanka, and the nation's coastal areas are critical breeding grounds for them. There are several well-known turtle species found in Sri Lanka.

  • The Green Turtle
  • Loggerhead Turtle
  • Hawksbill Turtle
  • Olive Ridley Turtle
  • Leatherback Turtle

    • Threats to the turtle population in Sri Lanka

    It is crucial to stress the fact that turtle populations all around the world, including those in Sri Lanka, are under risk for a variety of reasons. Some of these concerns include poaching, habitat damage, pollution, pollution from fishing gear, and climate change.The conservation initiatives, like as protected nesting grounds, community engagement, and public awareness campaigns, are extremely important for Sri Lanka's turtle populations.

    How to get More information

    I advise contacting current scientific studies, conservation groups, or government organizations focused on sea turtle conservation in Sri Lanka for the most up-to-date information on the state of the nation's turtle population.
