Hikkaduwa

The hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a conservation institution located in Sri Lanka's beach resort of Hikkaduwa. It is a well-liked tourist destination that lays a lot of emphasis on educating people about sea turtles as well as preserving and protecting these interesting animals.     • Educational Programs

    The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery educates visitors about the life cycle, behavior, and conservation status of sea turtles through interactive exhibits and educational activities. Visitors may discover the dangers that these species face and how they can help to ensure their preservation.

    Release Programs

    Participating in the turtle release program at the hatchery is one of the most memorable experiences there. Visitors have the opportunity to give young turtles a safe start to their adventure by releasing them into the sea.

    Guided Tours

    The hatchery offers educated staff-led guided tours that give you insightful information about the work being done and address any concerns you may have. The tour guides also stress the value of responsible travel and the contribution that each person can make to the preservation of sea turtles.
