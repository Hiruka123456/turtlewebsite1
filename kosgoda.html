Kosgoda Turtle hatchery

Another well-known conservation facility is the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, which is located in the Sri Lankan coastal community of Kosgoda. It concentrates on the preservation and protection of sea turtles and gives visitors the chance to learn about these amazing animals, just like the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery does.

  • Guided Tours and Educational Programs
  • Rehabilitation Center
  • Visitor Center and Souvenir Shop

    • Guided Tours and Educational Programs

    The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery offers expert tour guides who offer insights into the conservation work being done at the facility throughout their guided tours. Visitors may discover more about the sea turtle's life cycle, habits, and dangers. The purpose of educational programs is to increase visitors' knowledge of conservation concerns and encourage them to support turtle protection.

    Rehabilitation Center

    For ill or wounded turtles, the hatchery also functions as a rehabilitation facility. Visitors could get to observe turtles receiving medical attention and rehabilitation, learning more about the recovery process and ultimate release back into their natural environment.

    Visitor Center and Souvenir Shop

    Typically, the hatchery has a visitor center where you may find out more about the center's programs and how crucial turtle conservation is. Additionally, there is sometimes a gift shop where you may buy things related to turtles, with the money going toward the conservation initiatives of the hatchery.
